# Ping View

Client-side mod that shows your and other players ping in the tab list with color, simple as that.
Designed to keep the tab list looking as close to vanilla as possible.

![Tab](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/8ceb4bf0ca5bb67aee764c5b579a65f179afc723.png)

See your own and other players ping in the tab list, with color-coded values for easy reading.
- Color-coded ping based on latency
- Designed to keep the tab list as close to vanilla as possible
- Lightweight and works out of the box
